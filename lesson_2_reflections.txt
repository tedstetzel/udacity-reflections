What are some situations when branches would be helpful in keeping your history organized? How would branches help?
Branches would help if you are creating a new feature that may or may not land on the master branch. Also if you have a feature that is going to take a long time to build and you or someone on your team needs to make small maintenance updates to master, then it would be helpful to test new features on a new branch.
